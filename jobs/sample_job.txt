Summary

Legendary Entertainment is seeking an intern for its Accounting department. This individual will report directly to the SVP of Corporate Accounting and work together with the Finance team. In addition, the intern will work closely with the executives and members of the team.

Responsibilities

Compile and analyze financial information and prepare well-documented journal entries for approval by the Accounting team.
Record journal entries for the Accounting team.
Prepare schedules to facilitate the close.
Track royalty reports received and generate invoices.
Interact with other divisions/departments in a timely, courteous, and professional manner.
Complete special projects as assigned.
Other duties as required.

Requirements

Must be enrolled in a college/university taking at least one class in the semester/quarter (Spring/Summer) prior to participation in the internship program OR must have graduated from a college/university within the past six (6) months
A GPA of 3.5 or above is highly preferred
Must be at least 18 years of age
Must possess unrestricted work authorization
Undergraduate accounting major, preferred
High level of oral/written communication, interpersonal skills, and attention to detail
Strong organization, time management, and ability to prioritize tasks
Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, draw valid conclusions, and recommend effective solutions
Some knowledge or willingness to learn reporting standards and research accounting issues
Strong computer skills including proficiency with Microsoft Excel, Word, and PowerPoint
